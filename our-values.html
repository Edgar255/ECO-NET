OUR MISSION
We seek to provide easy access to products that support our commitment to protect the environment, reduce waste, and strive for sustainability. We believe in the power of making good choices that consider surrounding relationships with other living organisms and our shared habitat.

At Ecological Market we make it our mission to help connect you to lower impact daily items. Everyday we are inundated with single-use plastics, harmful chemicals, and unethically sourced household items. A lot of these items are very difficult to avoid and eliminating impact completely is near impossible. Our collective use has had a devastating impact on our planet. Luckily there are a lot of creative entrepreneurs out there with our shared vision. Exciting new solutions are popping up every day and we can’t wait to find them and make them available to you. This is where the fun part begins!

We search to find the best alternative low impact solutions centered around everyday living. We seek the brands that share our core values. Whether it’s replacing single-use plastics with reusable solutions, eliminating harmful chemicals, or choosing to shop fair trade we aim to be your go-to source for discovering all the new ways to make zero waste living easier and more enjoyable.

ECO FRIENDLY
First and foremost we consider the environmental impact of every product we carry. We seek products that utilize sustainable nontoxic materials and are produced without the destruction of precious natural resources. More specifically we are focused on providing products that help eliminate trash and promote a harmonious relationship with our environment.

ZERO WASTE
We prioritize products that use natural materials and are ecologically sound. We evaluate the impact of a product from production to disposal. We try to avoid plastic as much as possible and choose products that can be safely recycled or composted. We are fully committed to being part of eliminating single-use plastic waste worldwide and we will only ship with compostable or recyclable materials.

FAIR TRADE
Fair Trade standards provide a framework for sustainable, socially responsible, and ethical financial practices that work to improve lives and protect the environment. We are fully committed to sustainable economic development. We partner with companies that provide working communities with a fair wage, safe working conditions, and avoid child labor. We are 100% committed to the principles of Fair Trade.
